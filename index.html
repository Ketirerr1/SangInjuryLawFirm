About Me and My Journey

My name is Sang Min Lim. I was born in Seoul, South Korea and moved to the United States to study at Brown University. While I was there, I saw K-pop become popular in America, which inspired me to try a music career.

After earning my BA in Economics at Brown, I moved to Los Angeles to follow my love for music and later went to USC Law School. Balancing music and law taught me the value of truly connecting with people. That’s why I focus on a legal practice that puts clients first.

I passed three bar exams on my first try and am now admitted to practice in Arizona, California, Colorado, Nevada, New Mexico, Texas, and Washington.
